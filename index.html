ABOUT SHU

SHU rose from the concept of Loyalty and HOLDing.

The more $SHU you hold, the more you will earn in BNB's. And the opposite applies. As you sell your $SHU, you'll get taxed a disloyalty fee. Thus creating an ecosystem that encourages investors to hold on to their tokens and reap the rewards.

HOW TO BUY SHIBAUNCLE

  • Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

    Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that, you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

  • Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

    Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

  • Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for $SHU

    Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address (Address Will Be Updated Here After Presale Ends) to select SHU. Set the slippage tolerance to 11% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)

  • View $SHU and HODL

    Swap BNB for SHU. Now you need to add SHU to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) or Trust Wallet to view your $SHU. Lastly HODL!
